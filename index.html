Biography

Dr. Lei Xue is a research assistant professor in Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He also earned the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). Before his Ph.D. study, He received the Master and the Bachelor degree from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

Dr. Lei Xue is widely interested in designing and implementing efficient and practical security systems, with a particular focus on applying program analysis methodology to addressing the challenging security issues and detecting vulnerabilities in mobile and network systems. Currently, his research topics include mobile system security, IoT security, and telematics security.
