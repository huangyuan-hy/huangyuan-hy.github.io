Biography

Huang Yuan received the PhD degree in computer science from Sun Yat-sen University in 2017. He is an associate professor in the School of Software Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University. He was a research assistant at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He is particularly interested in intelligent software engineering, code summary technology, code defect prediction, blockchain, smart contract. He has published more than 30 papers in the software engineering journals and conferences, including: TSE, TOSEM, TR, EMSE, ASE, IST, JSS, JSCT, SPE, JSEP and FSE, ICPC, SANER, ESEM, etc..

Looking for highly motivated students in the areas of Software Engineering. Please contact me if you have interests.
